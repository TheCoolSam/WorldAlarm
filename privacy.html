Privacy Policy for TravelWorldAlarm

Effective Date: June 26, 2026

TravelWorldAlarm is built as a local utility application. We value your privacy and do not collect, store, or transmit any personal data.

1. Data Collection & Processing

Our app runs entirely client-side. We do not gather or store personal details. Below is how the app handles local information:

  • Alarms and Pinned Zones: All scheduled alarm dates, times, labels, and target timezones are saved locally on your device in a secure private database. We have no access to this database.
  • Contacts Listing (Optional): If you choose to link an alarm to a contact, the app requests local read-only access to your device's Address Book. The contact information remains entirely on your device and is never sent over the internet.

2. Location & Timezone Tracking

The app listens to system changes in timezone (e.g. when you land in a new country or travel to a new state). This check happens entirely on your device and does not track your location coordinates.

3. Children's Privacy

Since we do not collect any personal data, our application does not pose any risk to children or collect information from children under the age of 13.

4. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date.

5. Contact Us

If you have any questions or suggestions, please contact us directly via your developer profile support email.
